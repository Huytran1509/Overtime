The Badminton World Federation (BWF) has confirmed that New Delhi will host the 2026 BWF World Championships, bringing the event back to India after a gap of 17 years and returning it to Asia after four years. The announcement was made by the BWF and represents a major milestone for Indian badminton. New Delhi is set to welcome the world's top shuttlers later this year for what will be one of the most anticipated editions of the championships in recent memory.
