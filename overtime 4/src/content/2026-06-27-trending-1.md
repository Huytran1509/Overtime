The Washington Wizards used the first overall pick in the 2026 NBA Draft to select BYU freshman forward AJ Dybantsa, a 19-year-old, 6ft 9in prospect who averaged 25.5 points per game in his lone college season. Dybantsa, who also led the United States to the 2025 FIBA Under-19 World Cup title and was named tournament MVP, becomes the first BYU player ever taken with the top pick. Kansas guard Darryn Peterson and Duke's Cameron Boozer followed at picks two and three to Utah and Memphis, respectively.
