Serena Williams has accepted the final women's singles wild card for Wimbledon 2026, the All England Club confirmed on June 21, setting up her first singles appearance at a Grand Slam since the 2022 US Open. The 23-time Grand Slam champion, now 44, had already secured a doubles wild card alongside her sister Venus, meaning she will compete in both disciplines when Wimbledon begins on June 29. Williams' comeback has been gathering momentum after she returned to doubles competition at Queen's Club earlier this month.
