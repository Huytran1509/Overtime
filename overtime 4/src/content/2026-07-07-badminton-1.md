The BWF World Championships 2026 is confirmed to take place at the Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium in New Delhi from August 17 to 23, marking only the second time India has hosted the global badminton showpiece after Hyderabad in 2009. With the next major BWF World Tour event being the Super 750 Japan Open, elite players are gearing up for the second half of the season. The current men's singles world ranking leader is Indonesia's Alwi Farhan, who also claimed the Indonesia Masters title earlier this year.
