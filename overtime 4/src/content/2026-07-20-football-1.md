Ferran Torres scored the decisive goal after Argentina's Enzo Fernández was shown a red card in stoppage time. Spain's campaign was historically dominant, conceding only one goal across eight matches — the fewest ever by a World Cup champion. Rodri lifted the trophy at the ceremony, while a visibly emotional Lionel Messi received his runner-up medal in what may have been his last World Cup match.
