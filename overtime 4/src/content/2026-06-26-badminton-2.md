The 26th edition of the Badminton Asia Junior Championships kicked off on June 26 in Yatsushiro, Japan, with the mixed team event running through June 30 before individual events begin July 1. India's 19-member squad is led by Tanvi Patri and Abhinav Garg, who earned direct selection after winning the All India U-19 ranking tournament. India have historically won 11 medals at this championship, including individual golds by PV Sindhu and Lakshya Sen.
