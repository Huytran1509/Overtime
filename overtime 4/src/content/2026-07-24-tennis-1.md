The USTA has released the direct entry lists for the 2026 US Open, with two-time defending champion Aryna Sabalenka and Wimbledon winner Jannik Sinner topping their respective fields. Sabalenka, ranked world No. 1, will bid to become the first woman to win three consecutive US Open titles since Serena Williams achieved the feat between 2012 and 2014. Sinner, fresh from claiming his fifth major title at Wimbledon by defeating Alexander Zverev in the final, will attempt to reclaim the title he lost to Carlos Alcaraz last year. The main draw begins on August 30.
