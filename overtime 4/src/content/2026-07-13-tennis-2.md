Czech 21-year-old Linda Noskova became Wimbledon women's singles champion after defeating compatriot Karolina Muchova in three sets, claiming her first Grand Slam title. Noskova dominated the first set and held a dominant lead in the second before Muchova rallied to level, but the young player regrouped and closed out the third set to win. She becomes the 10th consecutive different women's champion at Wimbledon and the youngest women's winner at the tournament since Petra Kvitova in 2011.
