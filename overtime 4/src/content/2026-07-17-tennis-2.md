Czech teenager Linda Noskova claimed her first Grand Slam title at Wimbledon, defeating compatriot Karolina Muchova 6-2, 5-7, 6-3 in an all-Czech women's final. At 21, Noskova became the youngest Wimbledon women's champion since Petra Kvitova in 2011, having beaten five seeded players en route to the title. She is the tenth consecutive different women's Wimbledon champion, underlining the remarkable parity at the top of the women's game.
