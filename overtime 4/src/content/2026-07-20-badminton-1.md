India's PV Sindhu captured her maiden Japan Open title at the BWF World Tour Super 750 event in Tokyo, defeating world number three Akane Yamaguchi 21-17, 21-17 in straight games on July 19. The victory ended Sindhu's 19-month wait for a BWF World Tour title and marked her first Super 750 crown. The win also made her the first Indian player to claim the Japan Open women's singles title, arriving at an ideal time ahead of the upcoming BWF World Championships in New Delhi.
