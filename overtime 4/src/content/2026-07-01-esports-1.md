The 2026 Esports World Cup, the third edition of the annual multi-title tournament series, will hold its official opening ceremony at La Seine Musicale in Paris on July 8, ahead of competition running through August 23. For the first time, the event moves away from Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and will feature 25 competitions across 24 titles, including the return of Fortnite and the debut of Trackmania. The Club Championship offers a total prize pool of $30 million to the top 24 participating organizations.
