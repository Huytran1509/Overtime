India has placed three players in the semifinals of the 2026 Yonex US Open BWF Super 300 event in Fullerton, California. Young qualifier Rounak Chouhan reached his first-ever Super 300 semifinal by beating world No. 6 Misha Zilberman of Israel in straight games, 23-21, 21-11, in just 38 minutes. Veteran Kidambi Srikanth and women's singles contender Devika Sihag also progressed, with Sihag upsetting third-seeded Japanese player Riko Gunji in the quarterfinals to raise hopes of an all-Indian final.
