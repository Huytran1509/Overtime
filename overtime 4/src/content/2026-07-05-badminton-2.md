The 2026 Badminton Asia Junior Championships wrapped up its individual events stage today in Yatsushiro, Japan, with the tournament having run from June 26. India sent a 19-member squad led by Tanvi Patri and Abhinav Garg, both of whom were directly selected after winning the All India U-19 ranking tournament. The championships are the continent's premier junior competition, with India historically earning medals including individual golds from PV Sindhu and Lakshya Sen in past editions.
