The 26th edition of the Badminton Asia Junior Championships is taking place in Yatsushiro, Japan, with the team event running from June 26 to 30 and individual events from July 1 to 5. India's 19-member squad is led by Tanvi Patri and Abhinav Garg, both of whom qualified directly after winning the All India Under-19 ranking tournament. India has a strong history at the competition, having previously produced individual gold medallists including PV Sindhu in 2012 and Lakshya Sen in 2018.
