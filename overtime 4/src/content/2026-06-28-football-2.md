As of June 26, over 3.6 million spectators had attended matches across the United States, Canada, and Mexico, with the final number set to grow until the tournament concludes on July 19 in New Jersey. The expanded 48-team format, spread across 16 stadiums in three host nations, has been credited with driving the record-breaking crowds.
