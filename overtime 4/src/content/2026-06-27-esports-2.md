Riot Games has announced a partnership with the streaming platform KICK, beginning with MSI 2026, to carry live broadcasts for League of Legends, VALORANT, and TFT esports events globally, excluding China and Korea. The deal is aimed at reaching more fans in regions such as LATAM, MENA, and Europe, where KICK has a strong user base. The partnership also opens new co-streaming and creator program opportunities for KICK streamers.
