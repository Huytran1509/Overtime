The United States Tennis Association released the direct entry lists for the 2026 US Open, with Wimbledon champion Jannik Sinner and two-time defending champion Aryna Sabalenka leading the respective men's and women's fields. Sinner arrives at Flushing Meadows off the back of his second Wimbledon title, where he defeated Alexander Zverev in the final to claim his fifth Grand Slam. Sabalenka will seek to become the first woman to win three consecutive US Open titles since Serena Williams achieved the feat from 2012 to 2014. Carlos Alcaraz, who missed Wimbledon with a wrist injury, hopes to return during the North American hard-court swing.
