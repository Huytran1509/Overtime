Czech youngster Linda Noskova, 21, claimed her first Grand Slam title at Wimbledon, defeating compatriot Karolina Muchova 6-2, 5-7, 6-3 on Centre Court. The victory makes Noskova the youngest women's Wimbledon champion since Petra Kvitova in 2011 and the tenth consecutive different women's winner at the tournament. Muchova, making her second Grand Slam final appearance, pushed hard in the second set but could not prevent Noskova from closing it out.
