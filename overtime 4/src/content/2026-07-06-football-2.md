FIFA's Disciplinary Committee suspended the one-game ban handed to US striker Folarin Balogun after reviewing his red card from the Round of 32 win over Bosnia-Herzegovina, clearing him to play in Monday's Round of 16 against Belgium in Seattle. Balogun had been sent off in the second half after a VAR-reviewed challenge, but the ruling was overturned ahead of the high-stakes fixture. The Belgian Football Association released a statement expressing their reaction to the decision. The USMNT learned of the reversal alongside the broader public.
