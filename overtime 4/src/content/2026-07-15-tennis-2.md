Czech teenager Linda Noskova won the Wimbledon women's singles title, defeating compatriot Karolina Muchova 6-2, 5-7, 6-3 in an all-Czech Centre Court final. At 21, Noskova is the youngest women's Wimbledon champion since Petra Kvitova in 2011 and became the tenth consecutive different women's winner at the All England Club. Noskova had multiple championship points in the second set before Muchova rallied to force a decider.
