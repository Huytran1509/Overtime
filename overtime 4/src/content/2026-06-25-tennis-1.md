Former Wimbledon champion Marketa Vondrousova has been handed a four-year suspension by the International Tennis Integrity Agency after an independent tribunal ruled she refused an out-of-competition doping test at her home in December 2025. The 26-year-old Czech player, who won Wimbledon in 2023, denied ever doping, saying the testing officer arrived late at night without properly identifying themselves. Her suspension runs until June 21, 2030, and critics have drawn uncomfortable comparisons to far shorter bans handed to Jannik Sinner and Iga Swiatek after positive tests.
