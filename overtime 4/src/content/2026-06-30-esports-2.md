The 2026 Esports World Cup has been relocated from its usual home in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, to Paris, France, where it will be held at the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles from July 6 to August 23. The move was made in response to regional instability in the Middle East. The event will feature 25 tournaments across 24 different esports titles and carries a total prize pool of 75 million US dollars, surpassing last year's record.
