The Badminton World Federation, through the Badminton Association of India, launched a mascot competition on July 14 for the upcoming BWF World Championships 2026. New Delhi will host the global badminton showpiece, marking only the second time India has staged the event after Hyderabad in 2009. The competition invites submissions ahead of what is set to be a landmark edition of the world's premier individual badminton event.
