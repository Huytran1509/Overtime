Malaysia's Goh Jin Wei secured a place in the 2026 Thailand Masters women's singles final after defeating compatriot Wong Ling Ching in a hard-fought three-game battle. Her appearance in the final marks a historic milestone, as she becomes the first Malaysian women's singles player to reach the Thailand Masters final since the Super 300 format was introduced in 2016. She will face India's Devika Sihag, who overcame fifth seed Huang Yu-Hsun in the other semi-final.
