Jannik Sinner successfully defended his Wimbledon men's singles title by defeating Alexander Zverev 6-7, 7-6, 6-3, 6-4 in the final on July 12, claiming his fifth Grand Slam overall. Sinner dropped the opening tiebreaker before winning the next three sets in a match that featured a memorable 23-shot rally on the championship point. Zverev became the first German man to contest the Wimbledon final since Boris Becker in 1995.
