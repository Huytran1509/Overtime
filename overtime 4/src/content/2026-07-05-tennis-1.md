Philippine 21-year-old Alexandra Eala defeated defending Wimbledon champion Iga Swiatek 7-6 (11-9), 6-2 on Centre Court, becoming the first Filipino player in the Open Era to reach the second week of a Grand Slam. Eala saved multiple set points in a titanic first-set tiebreak before dominating the second set, ending Swiatek's title defence and dropping the Pole from third to sixth in the world rankings. She will next face 13th seed Jasmine Paolini for a place in the quarterfinals.
