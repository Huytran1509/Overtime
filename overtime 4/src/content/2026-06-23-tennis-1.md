The All England Club confirmed on June 21 that Serena Williams has accepted a singles wildcard for Wimbledon 2026, marking her first singles appearance at a Grand Slam since the 2022 US Open. The 44-year-old seven-time Wimbledon singles champion is also set to play doubles alongside her sister Venus Williams, who received a separate wildcard. Williams had been easing back into competitive tennis by playing doubles at Queen's Club and the Berlin Open earlier in the grass-court season. Wimbledon begins June 29 and the singles draw is scheduled for Friday.
