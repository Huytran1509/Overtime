The 26th Badminton Asia Junior Championships are taking place in Yatsushiro, Japan, with the mixed team event running from June 26 to 30, followed by individual events from July 1 to 5. India's squad is led by rising stars Tanvi Patri and Abhinav Garg, both of whom were directly selected after winning the All India Under-19 ranking tournament. India have historically won 11 medals at this event, including individual golds by PV Sindhu and Lakshya Sen.
