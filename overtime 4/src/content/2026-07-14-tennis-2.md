Czech 21-year-old Linda Noskova claimed her first Grand Slam singles title on July 11, defeating compatriot Karolina Muchova 6-2, 5-7, 6-3 in an all-Czech women's final at Wimbledon. Noskova became the youngest Wimbledon women's champion since Petra Kvitova in 2011, and the third different Czech woman in four years to win the title. The final was also the first all-same-nation women's final at the All England Club since the 2009 all-American showdown between Serena and Venus Williams.
