China's Hu Zhe'an and South Korea's Kim Ga-eun were crowned singles champions at the recently concluded Sands China Macau Open 2026, a BWF World Tour Super 300 event. Hu defeated Thailand's Kantaphon Wangcharoen in three sets to secure his maiden Macau Open title, while Kim beat compatriot Park Ga-eun in straight games in an all-Korean women's final. The tournament carried a total prize fund of $370,000 and was held at the East Asian Games Dome in Cotai.
