The opening ceremony of the 2026 National Youth Badminton Championship – Donex Cup took place on the evening of June 21 at Tien Son Sports Palace in Da Nang. The tournament, running from June 22 to 27, attracted nearly 600 athletes from 27 provinces and cities across Vietnam. Players compete across five age groups from U9 to U17 in multiple event formats.
