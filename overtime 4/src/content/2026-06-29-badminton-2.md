The 26th edition of the Badminton Asia Junior Championships is being held in Yatsushiro, Japan, from June 26 to July 5. The team event portion runs through June 30, with individual events beginning on July 1. India sent a 19-member squad led by Tanvi Patri and Abhinav Garg, both of whom qualified after winning the All India Under-19 ranking tournament.
