Indian teenager Rounak Chouhan, ranked 80th in the world, produced a major upset at the US Open 2026 badminton tournament in California by defeating top seed Chou Tien Chen of Chinese Taipei. The 18-year-old, who entered through qualifying, won 21-17, 26-24 in a match lasting 49 minutes, saving four game points to clinch the second game. Chouhan had only made his BWF World Tour debut the previous week at the Macau Open, where he also reached the pre-quarters stage.
